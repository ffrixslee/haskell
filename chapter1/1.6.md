Intermission: Equivalence exercises

alpha equivalence and how multiple heads are nested, choose
an answer that is equivalent to the listed lambda term:
1. 𝜆𝑥𝑦.𝑥𝑧
a) 𝜆𝑥𝑧.𝑥𝑧

2. 𝜆𝑥𝑦.𝑥𝑥𝑦
